E-Commerce Customer Behavior & Sales Analysis

A Power BI dashboard project focused on analyzing customer behavior, sales performance, revenue trends, and business KPIs to support data-driven decision-making.

Project Overview

This dashboard provides actionable insights into customer purchasing patterns, product performance, payment preferences, and sales trends. Using Power BI, the project transforms raw business data into interactive visualizations that help identify growth opportunities and improve business performance.
